Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

BreathNow+ ("the App") is a free breathing exercise application. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we handle and how.

Our Core Principle

BreathNow+ does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app works entirely on your device with no server-side components, no accounts, and no tracking.

Apple HealthKit

BreathNow+ can optionally integrate with Apple Health to save your breathing sessions as Mindful Minutes. This is entirely optional and requires your explicit permission.

  • What we save: Mindful session duration (start time and end time)
  • Where it's stored: Locally on your device in Apple Health. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.
  • Your control: You can enable or disable HealthKit access at any time in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access → BreathNow+

In compliance with Apple's HealthKit guidelines, we do not use HealthKit data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose other than providing health-related functionality to you.

Local Preferences

The app stores your preferences (such as session duration and sound settings) locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data is never transmitted anywhere.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No account registration or login
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • No location data
  • No crash reporting to external services
  • No cookies or web tracking
  • No third-party SDKs or frameworks that collect data

Third-Party Services

BreathNow+ does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app contains no third-party SDKs that collect user data.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties. There is no data to share because we do not collect any.

Children's Privacy

BreathNow+ does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Links to Other Apps

BreathNow+ may contain a link to Mind Well on the Apple App Store. If you tap this link, you will leave BreathNow+ and be directed to the App Store, which is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and reflected in the "Last updated" date above. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
